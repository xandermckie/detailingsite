Common Questions

FAQ

Do you take payment through the site?
No — payment is always handled in person on the day of the detail. No card information is collected anywhere on this site.
How long will my detail take?
Minimum three hours, often more depending on vehicle size and condition. We don't rush. If it needs more time, it gets more time.
Do you work on all vehicle types?
Yes — cars, trucks, SUVs, and vans. Pricing is per vehicle regardless of size. Mention anything unusual in your booking notes.
What if I have heavy staining?
We work stains as part of the standard detail. Let us know in advance and we'll address it. Not every stain is removable but we always give it a proper attempt.
When are you available?
Weekends only — Saturdays and Sundays. 8 AM, 10 AM, and 12 PM start times. Slots are limited so book ahead if you can.
What's included in the engine cleaning?
We degrease and clean the accessible engine bay — removing built-up grime and leaving it presentable. Included in the standard package.

The Story

Aaron & Xander

This started before I could even reach the roof of a car. My dad Aaron has been doing this for as long as I can remember, and growing up I was always right there next to him learning. You don't realize it at the time, but that's where the standard gets set — watching someone who actually takes pride in the work.

We started doing details together a few years ago and haven't stopped. Most of what we've done has been for family and close friends. People we know, cars we care about. That's still mostly who we do it for. When you're working on a car that belongs to someone you'll see at the dinner table, you don't cut corners. You do the whole job.

Over ten years of this means we've seen everything. Cars that looked like they'd never been cleaned a day in their lives. Interiors that were genuinely rough. Engine bays that hadn't been touched since the car was purchased. We handle all of it the same way — by hand, with real attention, and without looking at the clock.

We're not a shop. There's no franchise behind us, no production line pushing us through cars. It's just the two of us, and we treat every vehicle like it belongs to someone we know. Because it usually does.

We've kept it small and honest intentionally. We'd rather do fewer details and do them right than run a volume operation that isn't actually proud of the results. That's always been the standard, and it's not changing.
